The Newcastle Pet Cremation Difference

Why families across the North East trust us.

Individual, Never Mixed

When you choose individual cremation, your pet is cremated alone. The ashes you receive are theirs and theirs alone. We follow a strict traceability protocol so you never have to wonder.

No Rush, No Pressure

You'll never be hurried. We give you time to say goodbye at your own pace. Our team handles everything — from collection to hand-delivery of the ashes.

Collection from Your Home or Vet

We collect from your home, your veterinary practice, or you can bring your companion to us. Collection is available across Newcastle, Gateshead, North Tyneside, and throughout the North East.

Serving the North East

We serve families throughout:

  • Newcastle upon Tyne
  • Gateshead
  • North Tyneside
  • South Tyneside
  • Northumberland
  • Sunderland
  • Durham and Chester-le-Street
  • Hexham and the Tyne Valley

Collection is typically within 2 hours in Newcastle and 4 hours in wider Northumberland and County Durham.
