Memorial Products

Beautiful ways to keep your companion close — hand-finished with care in the North East.

🪵

Hand-Finished Wooden Caskets

Solid oak-effect caskets included as standard with individual cremation. Upgrade to a solid oak casket for £35 — hand-turned by a local woodworker, with a smooth, warm finish. Suitable for display at home or burial.

Included (standard) or +£35 (solid oak)

🖐

Paw-Print Keepsake

A clay impression of your companion's paw — taken with care before cremation, then kiln-fired and finished with your companion's name and dates. A tangible, permanent reminder.

£25

💍

Memorial Jewellery

A sterling silver pendant that holds a tiny trace of ash — sealed invisibly within the piece. Wear them close to your heart, always. Subtle and elegant — no one needs to know what it holds unless you tell them.

£55

📜

Scatter Tube

A biodegradable tube for scattering ashes in a favourite place — a walk you loved, a garden they slept in, a view they barked at. Included as standard; choose scattering instead of a casket at no extra cost.

Included

🏷

Engraved Brass Plaque

A polished brass plaque engraved with your companion's name and chosen dates. Can be fixed to a casket, mounted on a garden stone, or kept separately. Hand-engraved by a local artisan.

£30

🖼

Photo Frame with Paw-Print

A double-aperture frame — one side for your favourite photo, one side for the paw-print keepsake. Hand-finished wood. A complete memorial piece for your home.

£40

Made Locally, With Care

We work with small, independent craftspeople in the North East — not mass-produced imports. Every piece is made to order with attention and respect.

No Pressure — Ever

You don't have to decide about memorial products when you first call us. Many families choose later, after the cremation, when they've had time to think. We keep ash samples for memorial jewellery and can take paw prints at any point before the cremation.

If you change your mind, just let us know. If you'd like to add something later, that's fine too.

Can't See What You Want?

If you have something specific in mind — a particular wood, an inscription, a custom piece — talk to us. Our local craftspeople can usually accommodate special requests and we'll quote you honestly before any work begins.

Want to Add a Memorial?

You can choose memorial products when you arrange the cremation — or any time afterwards. Just let us know.
