Memorial Products

Beautiful ways to keep your companion close — hand-finished with care in the North East.

Why Memorial Products Matter

When you lose a companion, their physical presence disappears from your home — the sound of their paws, the weight of them on the sofa, the familiar nudge of a nose. Memorial products bridge that gap. They give you something to hold, to look at, to touch — a tangible reminder that your companion was real and the bond you shared matters.

Every piece we offer is made by independent craftspeople in the North East — not mass-produced imports. Whether it's a hand-turned oak casket, a paw-print keepsake, or a sterling silver pendant, each item is crafted with the same care we'd want for our own companions.

You don't need to decide straight away. Many families choose memorial products days or even weeks after the cremation. We keep ash samples for jewellery and can take paw prints at any point before the cremation. Take your time — there's no rush, no pressure, and certainly no script.

When to Order Memorial Products

You can request memorial products at any stage of the cremation process — there's no deadline and no pressure to decide immediately.

  • ✓ When you first contact us — let us know which items you'd like alongside the cremation arrangement
  • ✓ After the cremation — many families take a few days or weeks before they're ready to think about keepsakes. We keep ash samples so you can order memorial jewellery later
  • ✓ Months later — if a birthday, anniversary, or Christmas sparks the desire for a permanent memorial, we're still here. Just reach out

Made Locally, With Care
