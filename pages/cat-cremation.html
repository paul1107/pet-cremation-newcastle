Cat Cremation in Newcastle upon Tyne

A gentle, unhurried farewell for your cat. Individual cremation, smaller caskets, optional paw-print keepsake — arranged with you.

For Cat Owners

Cats weave themselves into every corner of our lives — the morning nudge, the evening purr, the quiet companionship that asks for nothing but gives everything. When they're gone, the silence can feel unbearable.

Our cat cremation service in Newcastle provides a gentle, unhurried farewell. We take the practical side off your hands so you can take whatever time you need.

What's included with every cat cremation:

  • Collection from your home or vet — arranged at a time that suits you
  • Individual cremation in a dedicated chamber
  • Unique identification disc through every stage
  • Ashes returned in a smaller, hand-finished casket — or a scatter tube / keepsake urn if you prefer
  • Paw-print keepsake in clay, often included at no extra cost (just ask)
  • Certificate of individual cremation
  • Hand-delivery or collection of the ashes, arranged with you

Common Questions from Cat Owners

My cat died at the vet — what now?

A participating crematorium may collect directly from your vet's practice. Local vet practices may already know established providers and can liaise directly on your behalf.

Can I scatter the ashes in my garden?

Yes. Many cat owners choose to scatter ashes in a favourite garden spot or a quiet place their cat loved. We can return the ashes in a scatter tube if you'd prefer.

Is communal cremation cheaper?

Communal cremation is usually the most economical option — ashes are typically scattered in the crematorium's memorial garden rather than returned. Many cat owners choose individual cremation because the cost is similar and the ashes come back.

Do you have a memorial garden?

Yes — a peaceful, tended space for communal ashes. Individual ashes are returned to you, but you're welcome to visit the garden at any time.

Can I have ashes split between family members?

Yes. We can divide the ashes into smaller keepsake urns so different family members can each keep a portion. This is something we can help you arrange with you when you enquire.

What about memorial jewellery?

We can arrange pendants that hold a small trace of your cat's ashes. Many owners find this a quiet, personal way to keep their cat close. See options →
