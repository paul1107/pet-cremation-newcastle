What Happens Next

When you reach out, here's what to expect.

📞

1. We Call You Back

Usually within 30 minutes. We'll listen, answer your questions, and talk through the options — no pressure, no rush.

🚗

2. We Collect

We arrange collection from your home, vet, or you bring your companion to us. Typically within 2 hours in Newcastle.

🏠

For individual cremation, ashes are returned within 2–3 days in your choice of casket or scatter tube. Hand-delivered with care.
