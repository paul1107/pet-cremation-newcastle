What Happens Next

When you reach out, here's what to expect.

📞

1. A Crematorium Calls You

We connect you with a trusted local crematorium. They'll call you back — usually within the hour — to talk through the options. No pressure, no rush.

🚗

2. They Arrange Collection

Collection from your home, vet, or you can bring your companion in. Quick collection, often on the same day — arranged at a time that suits you.

🏠

3. Ashes Returned with Care

For individual cremation, ashes are hand-delivered to your door in your choice of casket or scatter tube — timing is arranged with you, typically within seven days.
