Tell us about your companion. We will review your enquiry and, if a participating provider is available, tell you who it is before asking whether you want a referral.

Thank You

We've received your enquiry. This site is an independent referral service, not a crematorium. We will review the information and contact you about provider availability; no provider receives your details until you agree.

What Happens Next

The referral process is deliberately transparent.

📝

1. We Receive Your Enquiry

Your details are sent to the site operator so we can understand what information or service area you are asking about.

🔎

2. We Check Availability

We check whether a participating local provider is available. We do not promise a provider, price or response time before confirmation.

🤝

3. You Decide

We tell you who is available and ask whether you want your details passed to that provider. The provider then explains its own collection, cremation and ash-return terms.

Need Help Understanding the Options?

Complete the form and we will review your enquiry. For urgent arrangements, contact an established local crematorium or your vet directly.
