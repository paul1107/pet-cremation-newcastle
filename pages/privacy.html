Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who We Are

Newcastle Pet Cremation Services ("we", "us", "our") provides pet cremation services to families across Newcastle upon Tyne and the North East of England. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with the same care and discretion we bring to every aspect of our service.

What Information We Collect

When you contact us, we may collect:

  • Contact details: your name, phone number, email address
  • Service details: your companion's type, size, and the service you're interested in
  • Collection preferences: where you'd like your companion collected from
  • Any additional information you choose to share in the message field

Contact Form Submissions — How We Process Your Data

When you submit our contact form, you are consenting to us processing your personal data for the purpose of connecting you with a trusted local pet cremation service provider ("crematorium"). Here's exactly what happens:

  • We receive your enquiry via our secure form handler
  • We share your details (name, phone number, email if provided, companion details, collection preference, and any message you've written) with one trusted crematorium in your area — the one best suited to your needs
  • That crematorium contacts you directly — usually within 30 minutes — with pricing and availability
  • We do not sell your data to multiple providers, data brokers, or anyone else
  • We do not use your data for marketing — no newsletters, no follow-ups beyond what's needed to ensure you were connected

Legal basis for processing: Your explicit consent (GDPR Article 6(1)(a)), given by ticking the consent checkbox on our contact form. You can withdraw consent at any time by contacting us at care@petcremationnewcastle.co.uk.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to:

  • Arrange and provide cremation services
  • Communicate with you about your companion's cremation and ash return
  • Process payment after the service is complete
  • Maintain records required for our business operations

We do not use your information for marketing. We do not send newsletters, promotional emails, or follow-up communications beyond what's necessary to complete the service you've asked us to provide. We will never sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain service records for 7 years — the standard period for business records in the UK. After this period, records are securely destroyed. Payment information is retained only as long as required for accounting purposes.

Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data. Our systems are secured, access is restricted to team members who need it to provide our service, and we regularly review our security practices.

Your Rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to our legal obligations to retain records)
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at care@petcremationnewcastle.co.uk or via our contact form.

Website

This website does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. It is a static site hosted on Vercel. We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook pixels, or any third-party tracking.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: care@petcremationnewcastle.co.uk
  • Online: Contact form
  • Post: Newcastle Pet Cremation Services, Newcastle upon Tyne, Tyne and Wear
