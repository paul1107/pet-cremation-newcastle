About Newcastle Pet Cremation

An independent information and referral service — not a crematorium.

Why This Site Exists

Choosing aftercare for a companion can be difficult, especially when you are making decisions under pressure. This site explains the difference between individual and communal cremation, common questions, and the information to ask a provider before booking.

Newcastle Pet Cremation is an independent referral website. It does not own a crematorium, collect pets, perform cremations or promise that a provider is available until that provider has confirmed it.

What We Believe

🤝

No Pressure. Ever.

We explain the options without asking you to commit to a product or provider. You remain free to compare and choose.

🔒

Total Traceability

Ask the crematorium you choose to explain its identification and traceability procedure, and what certificate or documentation it provides.

💛

Compassion Over Commerce

Any prices, payment terms, cancellation rules and opening hours belong to the provider carrying out the service. Ask for them clearly before you agree.

Who Operates the Site

This is a small independent referral project operated by Paul Clarke / Black and White Boy SEO. There is no Newcastle Pet Cremation collection team or crematorium behind this website. If a participating provider is available, we will tell you who it is before asking whether you want your details passed on.

Serving the North East

The site covers Newcastle upon Tyne, Gateshead, North Tyneside, South Tyneside, Sunderland, Northumberland, County Durham, and surrounding areas. Provider coverage and collection timing vary.

We do not claim that veterinary practices recommend this site. Families should ask their own vet which established aftercare providers they know and trust.

Need Clear Information?

If you've lost a companion — or you're preparing for the inevitable — use the form for information or to ask whether a participating provider is available. For urgent arrangements, contact an established crematorium or your vet directly.
