Paying Is Simple

💳

Card or Bank Transfer

Pay by debit card, credit card, or bank transfer. We'll send an invoice after the cremation — no payment before the service.

🗓

Pay Later — No Rush

We'll never chase payment on the day. We send an invoice afterwards and you settle it when you're ready. Grief is hard enough without worrying about money.

💚

No Hidden Costs

The price we quote is the price you pay. No surcharges, no extras you didn't ask for. If you change your mind about memorial products, just let us know.
