What Affects the Cost?

Pet cremation costs in Newcastle depend on a few key factors. Here's what matters — and why every situation is a little different. See our full cremation services guide for more on individual and communal options.

🐾

Your Companion's Size

The most significant factor. Smaller pets (rabbits, guinea pigs, birds) cost less because the cremation chamber is smaller and the process is shorter. Larger dogs require more time and a larger chamber.

💛

Individual vs Communal

Individual cremation — where your companion is cremated alone and their ashes returned to you — costs more than communal cremation, where ashes are scattered in a memorial garden. Most families choose individual.

🚗

Collection Distance

Collection within Newcastle, Gateshead, and the immediate Tyne & Wear area is typically included. Collection from further afield — Northumberland, County Durham — may carry a small additional fee.

Individual vs Communal — The Difference

Individual Cremation

Your companion is cremated alone in a clean chamber. Their ashes are collected separately and returned to you in a hand-finished casket or scatter tube, usually arranged with you, typically within seven days. You receive a certificate of individual cremation. This is the option most families choose. Learn about the cremation process →

Typically includes: collection, individual cremation, ashes returned in an oak-effect casket, and a certificate.

Communal Cremation

A dignified shared cremation. Your companion is cremated alongside other pets, and the ashes are scattered together in a memorial garden. You receive a certificate of cremation. A more affordable option for families who don't wish to keep the ashes.

Typically includes: collection, communal cremation, scattering in the memorial garden, and a certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions About Costs

Why can't I see exact prices?

Every crematorium sets its own prices, and costs vary by your companion's size, the type of cremation, and your location. We match you with a trusted local crematorium who'll give you a clear, personalised quote — no obligation. We believe in transparency, not one-size-fits-all pricing that turns out to be wrong.

Is there any obligation when I request a quote?

None at all. Fill in the form, a crematorium calls you back with their prices, and you decide. There's no pressure, no hard sell, and certainly no payment upfront.

Do I pay before or after the cremation?

Most crematoriums invoice after the service — you pay when you're ready. Grief is hard enough without worrying about money on the day.
