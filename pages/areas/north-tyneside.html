Pet Cremation in North Tyneside

Dignified, individual and communal cremation for families across North Tyneside — from the coast to the suburbs. Collection included.

The participating crematoriums serve families across North Tyneside — from Whitley Bay and Tynemouth on the coast to Wallsend, Killingworth, and Forest Hall inland. Quick collection, typically same-day — arranged with you, in almost all cases.

Fast, Local Collection

Collection arranged promptly across North Tyneside — from the coast to the A1 corridor. Same-day service available, 7 days a week.
