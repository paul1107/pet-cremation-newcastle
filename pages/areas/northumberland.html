Pet Cremation in Northumberland

Dignified, individual and communal cremation for families across Northumberland — from market towns to rural communities. Collection in 2–4 hours.

We serve families throughout Northumberland — from commuter towns like Ponteland and Cramlington to rural villages and farms. We're used to longer journeys and will collect wherever you are.

Rural Collection — No Problem

Collection within 2–4 hours across Northumberland. We're used to finding farm tracks, cottages up lanes, and rural postcodes. If a satnav can find you, so can we.

Individual cremation from £95 (small pets) to £250 (extra-large dogs) plus collection fee. Communal cremation from £45 plus collection.
