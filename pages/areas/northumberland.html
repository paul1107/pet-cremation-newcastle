Pet Cremation in Northumberland

Dignified, individual and communal cremation for families across Northumberland — from market towns to rural communities. Collection arranged to suit you, with typical same-day availability.

We serve families throughout Northumberland — from commuter towns like Ponteland and Cramlington to rural villages and farms. We're used to longer journeys and will collect wherever you are.

Rural Collection — No Problem

Collection arranged at a time that suits you across Northumberland. We're used to finding farm tracks, cottages up lanes, and rural postcodes. If a satnav can find you, so can we.

Individual cremation is priced by size — small pets are the most affordable, while extra-large dogs cost more (plus a small collection fee). Communal cremation is the most economical option. Complete the form for a personal quote including collection.
