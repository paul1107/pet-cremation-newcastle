Areas We Serve

Independent pet cremation information and referral for families across Newcastle upon Tyne, Tyneside and Northumberland. We describe options, then refer your enquiry to a participating provider once availability is confirmed and you agree.

Choose Your Area

Each page below explains what is typically available in that area, what to ask a participating provider, and how an enquiry is handled. No area is a guaranteed service — coverage, collection and pricing depend on the participating provider at the time of your enquiry.

  • Newcastle upon Tyne (NE1–NE7, NE12, NE13, NE15)
  • Gateshead (NE8–NE11)
  • North Tyneside — Whitley Bay, Tynemouth, North Shields, Wallsend
  • South Tyneside — South Shields, Jarrow, Hebburn
  • Northumberland — Morpeth, Hexham, Ponteland, Cramlington, Blyth

How the Referral Works

  1. You read what is typically available in your area.
  2. You complete the contact form with consent.
  3. We tell you which participating provider is available.
  4. You decide whether to share your enquiry with them.
  5. The provider contacts you directly to discuss collection, pricing and timing.

What We Don't Do

A clear operating model protects you and protects the site.

  • We do not collect, cremate or invoice. A participating provider does.
  • We do not publish invented testimonials, a fictional address, or a working phone number.
  • We do not share your details with any provider until you have agreed.
  • We do not quote a hard price or commit to a hard timing — those depend on the participating provider.

Frequently Asked

Is collection available in every area?

Collection availability depends on the participating provider on the day. Inner-city and Tyneside postcodes are usually covered; rural Northumberland may have longer lead times. We confirm availability with you before your details are shared with any provider.

Do you serve County Durham or Sunderland?

Our five listed areas are the focus. Some participating providers extend into County Durham and parts of Sunderland, but availability is not guaranteed. Tell us where you are in the contact form and we will tell you what is currently possible.

What if no provider is available today?

We will say so honestly. You are not committed to anything by submitting the form. If you have an urgent need, your vet can usually advise on a provider or local options.
