Individual Cremation

Your companion, cremated alone. Their ashes returned to you — theirs and theirs alone.

Individual cremation is the service most families choose. Your pet is cremated alone in a clean chamber. The ashes you receive are only your companion's — never mixed with others.

We follow a strict traceability protocol. Every pet is assigned a unique identification disc that travels with them through every stage. You never have to wonder whether the ashes coming home are really theirs.

What's Included

  • Collection from your home or vet (within 2 hours in Newcastle)
  • Individual cremation in a dedicated chamber
  • Your companion's ashes returned in a hand-finished wooden casket
  • Certificate of individual cremation
  • Hand-delivery or collection of the ashes within 2–3 days

Who Is This For?

Families who want their companion's ashes back — to keep at home, scatter in a favourite place, or divide among family members.

Common for:

  • Dogs of all sizes

Communal Cremation

A dignified farewell for families who do not wish to keep the ashes.

Who Is This For?

Families who want a respectful, dignified cremation but do not wish to keep the ashes. Perhaps you plan to remember your companion in other ways — photos, memories, a favourite walk.

Also commonly chosen for:

  • Smaller pets where individual ashes return isn't practical
  • Families on a tighter budget who still want a proper farewell
  • Those who'd prefer their companion's ashes scattered in our memorial garden

Arrange Communal Cremation

Your companion is cremated alongside a small number of other pets in a shared, dignified ceremony. Afterwards, the combined ashes are scattered with care in our memorial garden — a peaceful, tended space you're welcome to visit.

What's Included

  • Collection from your home or vet
  • Dignified communal cremation
  • Ashes scattered in our memorial garden
  • Certificate of communal cremation

No pressure, no upsell. We'll talk you through both options and you choose what feels right for your family.

Collection Service

We come to you — home, vet, or you bring your companion to us.

🏠

From Your Home

We collect from your home at a time that works for you. Typically within 2 hours in Newcastle, same-day in most cases. We arrive discreetly in an unmarked vehicle and handle everything with quiet dignity.

🏥

From Your Vet

If your companion passes at the vet, we can collect directly from the practice. Many local vets know us and call us directly — just let them know you'd like us to handle the cremation.

🤲

You Bring Them to Us

Some families prefer to bring their companion themselves. It can be an important part of saying goodbye. We'll arrange a private appointment so you have the time and space you need.

Not Sure Which Is Right for You?

We'll talk you through both options gently, answer every question, and help you decide — with no pressure at all.
