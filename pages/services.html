Pet Cremation Services in Newcastle upon Tyne

Individual and communal cremation — find the right farewell for your companion.

Individual Cremation

Individual cremation is the service most families choose. Your pet is cremated alone in a clean chamber. The ashes you receive are only your companion's — never mixed with others.

We follow a strict traceability protocol. Every pet is assigned a unique identification disc that travels with them through every stage. You never have to wonder whether the ashes coming home are really theirs.

What's Included

  • Collection from your home or vet (quick, often same-day — arranged with you)
  • Individual cremation in a dedicated chamber
  • Your companion's ashes returned in a hand-finished wooden casket
  • Certificate of individual cremation
  • Hand-delivery or collection of the ashes arranged with you, typically within seven days

Communal Cremation

A dignified farewell for families who do not wish to keep the ashes.

Who Is This For?

Families who want a respectful, dignified cremation but do not wish to keep the ashes. Perhaps you plan to remember your companion in other ways — photos, memories, a favourite walk.

Also commonly chosen for:

  • Smaller pets where individual ashes return isn't practical
  • Families on a tighter budget who still want a proper farewell
  • Those who'd prefer their companion's ashes scattered in our memorial garden

Arrange Communal Cremation

Your companion is cremated alongside a small number of other pets in a shared, dignified ceremony. Afterwards, the combined ashes are scattered with care in our memorial garden — a peaceful, tended space you're welcome to visit.

What's Included

  • Collection from your home or vet
  • Dignified communal cremation
  • Ashes scattered in our memorial garden
  • Certificate of communal cremation

No pressure, no upsell. We'll talk you through both options and you choose what feels right for your family.

Dog Cremation Newcastle

Dogs are family. From a Jack Russell who's been by your side for 14 years to a Great Dane who filled every room — they deserve a farewell that honours everything they gave you.

Our dog cremation service in Newcastle handles every breed and size. The process is the same careful, traceable service we provide for all pets, but we understand that losing a dog often means losing a daily presence — the morning walk, the greeting at the door, the quiet companion on the sofa.

We collect from your home or vet across Newcastle, Gateshead, North Tyneside, and the wider North East. Collection is typically same-day, and individual cremation ensures you receive only your dog's ashes back — in a casket or scatter tube of your choice.

Sizes we handle: Toy and small breeds (under 10 kg), medium breeds (10–25 kg), large breeds (25–45 kg), and extra-large breeds (45+ kg). Pricing is size-based — see our cost guide →

Common Questions from Dog Owners

  • How quickly can you collect? Same-day in almost all cases — arranged when you call.
  • Can my other dog say goodbye? Yes — many families find this helpful. We'll give you whatever time you need.
  • What about large breeds? We handle dogs of all sizes. Larger dogs require a bigger chamber — reflected in the pricing, which we'll quote honestly upfront.
  • Is individual cremation guaranteed? Yes — unique ID disc through every stage, certificate of individual cremation.

Cat Cremation Newcastle

Cats weave themselves into every corner of our lives — the morning nudge, the evening purr, the quiet companionship that asks for nothing but gives everything. When they're gone, the silence can feel unbearable.

Our cat cremation service in Newcastle provides a gentle, unhurried farewell. Individual cremation means your cat is cremated alone and their ashes returned to you — in a smaller, appropriately sized casket. We handle everything from collection to hand-delivery of the ashes.

Many cat owners choose to scatter ashes in a favourite garden spot or keep them in a small urn at home. We'll talk through the options with you — no rush, no pressure. See memorial options →

Collection Service
